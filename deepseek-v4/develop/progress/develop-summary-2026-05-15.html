Locked the V4-aware FLOP counter (within 1 %), removed silent nn.Linear fallbacks, wired core_attention for cr=0, and routed the Turbo DeepEP dispatcher into V4 specs.

Custom Triton Attention PLAN-4P24–P27 · 4 phases

Shipped Primus-owned Triton kernels for V4 attention: v4_attention (dense + HCA) and v4_csa_attention (sparse top-K), with full FWD + BWD parity gates at head_dim=512 release tier.

EP8 Proxy Perf Optimisation PLAN-5P28–P32 · 5 phases

Trace-anchored optimisation on the V4-Flash EP=8 proxy at S=4096: Sinkhorn reduce killed (P29), SWA K-loop pruning (P30), CSA in-kernel gather (P31), CSA BWD split (P31b), and CSA FWD split + dual-RoPE bf16 cast (P32 final) — 14.64× iter-time speed-up vs P28.

Small-Op & Layout Fusion PLAN-6P33–P40 · 8 phases

Closed the elementwise / layout tail surfaced by the P32 trace. 4 default-on Triton kernels: stack_grouped_weight (P34, −49.8 ms), apply_interleaved_partial_rope (P35, −5 ms), sinkhorn_normalize (P36, −11 ms), HyperMixer.compute_weights tail (P37, −3 ms). 2 opt-in: Indexer.forward (P38), v4_router_post (P39) — microbench wins but proxy-noise-bound; lm_loss bit-identical. P33 corrected the TFLOP/s denominator; P40 closed out with the cumulative bake-off.
